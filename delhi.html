delhi and new-delhi tour

a brief about delhi and new-delhi tour

Delhi is a treasure trove in history is the capital and heart of India. Bordered by Haryana and Uttar Pradesh it has been continuously inhabited since 6th Century BCE and has served as a capital to the various dynasties and kingdoms which had contributed it to make a thriving city where old and new blend seamlessly. It is a city of glorious past which has been captured and ransacked several times but has never lost its zest and hope and now emerged as a symbol of country’s rich cultural ... heritage and amazing traditions. With a splendid past and thriving present it is a home to millions of dreams and aspirations and people from all over the country come here to fulfil their ambitions and realizing their dreams. So pack your bags and book holiday packages to Delhi and enjoy a visit to colorful and vibrant city which is impossible to ignore. Delhi compels you to visit it again and again and there are several reasons of it as well. Its great historic background, tantalizing food, the contrasting character of Old and New Delhi, splendid architecture, amazing shopping options and rich cultural heritage etc are few among many. If you are history buff then it is very hard to ignore this magnetic city which was built several times under several kingdoms. Visit the ruined forts of Purana Qila, Tughlaqabad and Shajahanabad the might Red Fort, Qutub Minar, Humayun’s Tomb and capture the glimpses of amazing history of the city. The city represents a beautiful amalgamation of various religions and represents a true picture of secular India where you can find various temple, churches, mosques and shrines like Gurudwara Seesh Ganj, Laxmi Narayan Temple, Lotus Temple, Jama Masjid , Hazrat Nizamuddin Dargah etc are few among many. Delhi is a reservoir of rich culture and you can easily capture its glimpses by visiting Old Delhi. Congested souk like bazaars, maze of streets, amazing street food, ancient monuments, hundred years old havelis surely leave you awe-struck. On a visit to New Delhi you will find a complete contrast as it has modern sky rise buildings, wide avenues, beautiful colonial architecture, government buildings like President House, Parliament House, wide lush parks and green areas and great metro connectivity surely takes you to the different world altogether. Delhi is no less than a paradise for shopaholics. It has some of the best markets in the country and is quite famous for their unmatched variety where you can buy anything and everything. Do visit Sarojini Nagar, Lajpat Nagar, Chandni Chowk, Janpath, Karol Bagh etc which surely fascinates you with their amazing stuff. One of the strongest reasons to visit Delhi again and again is its tantalising food. The city has the most delicious street food and there are several famous restaurants like Karim, Halidiram, Bikanerwala, Nathu Sweets, Giani’s Parathe Wali Gali are some of the world –famous food outlets that offer you mouth-watering dishes that you just can’t resist. So if you don’t want to miss all this and much more then book Delhi Vacation package and enjoy a great holiday trip.
